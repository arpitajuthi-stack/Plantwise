About This Disease

Powdery mildew is a fungal disease that affects a wide range of plants. It appears as white or gray powdery spots on leaves, stems, and sometimes flowers.

💊 Treatment Suggestions
  • 1 Remove and dispose of affected leaves immediately
  • 2 Apply neem oil or fungicidal spray weekly
  • 3 Improve air circulation around the plant
  • 4 Avoid overhead watering to prevent spread
